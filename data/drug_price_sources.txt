Drug Price Data Sources
=======================
Last updated: 2026-04-13

Panel A drugs (Eliquis, Xarelto, Jardiance, Januvia, Entresto, Enbrel, Stelara,
Imbruvica, Farxiga):
  - US list prices, US net prices, and international prices (Australia, Canada,
    France, Germany, Switzerland, UK):
    Wouters OJ, Naci H, Kesselheim AS. Drug Prices Negotiated by Medicare
    Compared With US Net Prices and Prices in Other Countries. JAMA. 2025.
    PMC11612909. Table based on SSR Health net price methodology.
  - Medicare negotiated prices (IRA, effective 2026):
    CMS. Selected Drugs and Negotiated Prices. Medicare Drug Price Negotiation
    Program. https://www.cms.gov/priorities/medicare-prescription-drug-affordability

Ozempic, Wegovy (now replaced):
  - Peterson-KFF Health System Tracker. Prices of Drugs for Weight Loss in the
    US and Peer Nations. 2024.

Humira (now replaced):
  - World Population Review. Humira Cost by Country. 2023. Pre-biosimilar brand prices.

EpiPen:
  - World Population Review. EpiPen Price by Country. 2023.

Insulin (all types):
  - ASPE/HHS. Insulin Affordability and the Inflation Reduction Act. 2024.
    Gross manufacturer price per standard unit, all insulin types, 2022 data.

Imbruvica and Farxiga (new additions):
  - Same JAMA/Wouters et al. source as Panel A above.
  - US list and net prices from SSR Health methodology.
  - International prices from the same comparative dataset.

Estimated US net prices methodology:
  - For IRA-negotiated drugs: SSR Health database, which derives net prices from
    manufacturer SEC filings (10-K/10-Q). Reported in Wouters et al. JAMA 2025.
  - For Ozempic: estimated ~$650/month from multiple industry sources (Drug
    Channels, BCBS estimates). Moderate confidence.
  - For EpiPen: ~$170/injector derived from Mylan 2016 channel economics
    disclosure. Low confidence (outdated).
  - For Insulin (all types): ~$47/SU estimated at ~50% of list, consistent with
    industry average gross-to-net discounts. Moderate confidence.

Revenue rankings (2024 global sales):
  - Drug Discovery Trends. 2024's Blockbusters: Top 50 Pharmaceuticals by Sales.
  - BioSpace. 10 Best-Selling Drugs of 2024.

General gross-to-net context:
  - Drug Channels Institute. 2024 Gross-to-Net Realities at 9 Top Drugmakers.
  - Drug Channels Institute. U.S. Brand-Name Drug Prices Fell in 2025.
  - Industry average: brand drugs sell for ~50% of list after rebates/discounts.
