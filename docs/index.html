One Drug, Many Prices

Companion site for the working paper A Proposal to Reform US Healthcare Pricing.

This site hosts the full sources, derivations, and assumptions for every figure in the paper. Each number in the paper is traceable to its primary source here, with direct links to the underlying articles and data.
